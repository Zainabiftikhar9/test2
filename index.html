Meet Cat

Dog, (Canis lupus familiaris), domestic mammal of the family Canidae (order Carnivora). The dog is one of the two most ubiquitous and most popular domestic animals in the world (the cat is the other). For more than 12,000 years it has lived with humans as a hunting companion, protector, object of scorn or adoration, and friend.Dogs are regarded differently in different parts of the world

Meet Dog

Cats are the domestic mammals and subspecies of family Felidae. largest eye of any mammal. Their independent personality, grace, cleanliness, and subtle displays of affection have wide appeal. Typically, cats are creatures of habit; they are inquisitive, but not adventurous, and are easily upset by sudden changes of routine.
